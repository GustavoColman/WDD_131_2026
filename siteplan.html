site purpose

a online portifolio

  • The main idea of the website is to be an online portfolio that helps me connect with clients for drawing and 3D modeling commissions, as well as showcase my comic series. Although it is a college project, I intend to expand it further.

  • Why have my own website instead of using an online comic reading platform?

    Although an online reading platform has greater reach and popularity, with a structure already prepared to host webcomic authors, there is also a factor of instability in these platforms. Sites can shut down without prior notice, and servers can go down due to high traffic. Having a website to post my own work ensures that it won't be lost if a platform shuts down, and it also allows monetization through ads. Not to mention the competition with other comics, which can end up overshadowing my work.
